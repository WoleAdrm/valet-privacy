Privacy Policy for Valet - Professional Note Assistant

Last Updated: December 2024

Simple Summary: Valet does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Your notes stay private and secure in your browser.

Overview

Valet is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how our Chrome extension handles your information.

Data Collection

Valet does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

How Your Notes Are Stored

  • All notes are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage system
  • Notes are synced across your Chrome browsers through Google's secure sync infrastructure
  • No data is sent to external servers or third parties
  • We do not have access to your notes or any personal information

Permissions Used

  • activeTab: To display the notepad on websites you visit
  • scripting: To inject the notepad interface into web pages
  • storage: To save your notes locally and sync them across your Chrome browsers

Voice Recognition

  • Voice input uses your browser's built-in speech recognition
  • No audio data is transmitted to our servers
  • All voice processing happens locally in your browser

Data Security

  • Your notes remain within Google's Chrome ecosystem
  • We do not operate any servers that store user data
  • All data storage is handled by Chrome's secure storage APIs

Third Parties

  • Valet does not share data with third parties
  • No analytics or tracking services are used
  • No advertising networks have access to your data

Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted with an updated "Last Updated" date.
